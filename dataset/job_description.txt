Job Title: Data Analyst
Company: NovaTech Analytics — a fast-growing SaaS analytics company

We are seeking a skilled Data Analyst to join our Business Intelligence team.
You will work closely with product, marketing, and operations teams to transform raw data into actionable insights.

Requirements:
- Proficiency in Python and SQL for data analysis and querying
- Experience with Excel and Google Sheets for reporting
- Knowledge of Power BI or Tableau for dashboard development
- Understanding of Machine Learning concepts and statistical analysis
- Familiarity with Cloud platforms (AWS or GCP preferred)
- Experience with data cleaning, data wrangling, and ETL pipelines
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Ability to manage projects and meet deadlines (Project Management)
- Experience with Git version control
- Knowledge of statistics, regression, and predictive modeling

Preferred Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, or related field
- 2+ years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Familiarity with R or Scala
- Experience with Apache Spark or big data tools
- Strong problem-solving and critical thinking skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication

We value candidates who are curious, detail-oriented, and passionate about data-driven decision-making.
